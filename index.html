Yes. We've migrated from Epic, eClinicalWorks, athenahealth, Practice Fusion, and dozens of others. If it stores data, we can get it out.

Month-to-month means you can leave anytime. But with 95%+ retention, we're confident you'll stay.

Enterprise-grade security with SOC 2 compliance, end-to-end encryption, and regular third-party audits. Your data is safer with us than on paper.

Training is included and unlimited. We train until your team is confident, and we're always available for refreshers.

Yes. We support all major lab interfaces, e-prescribing (including controlled substances), and connect via FHIR/HL7 with most health systems.
