Subjective / HPI
Patient is a 42-year-old female presenting with a 3-month history of a changing lesion on her left upper back. She reports the lesion has grown approximately 3mm and developed asymmetric borders. Denies pain or bleeding. Family history of melanoma (mother).
Assessment
1. Atypical melanocytic lesion, left upper back — irregular borders, diameter ~7mm. ABCDE criteria: asymmetry, border irregularity present. Dermoscopy performed.
2. Rule out melanoma — biopsy indicated.
Plan
1. Excisional biopsy left upper back today — CPT 11102.
2. Pathology to Dermatopathology Lab — H&E and Melan-A staining.
3. Follow up in 10 days for results review.
4. Patient counseled on sun protection and self-exam.
