Our Story

Perry Software was born from frustration. After watching medical practices struggle with bloated, expensive EHR systems that seemed designed to make work harder — not easier — we decided to build something better.

Based in Lehi, Utah, we're a small team with a simple mission: create practice management software that medical professionals actually enjoy using, at a price that makes sense for independent practices.

Why We're Different

Big EHR companies have thousands of customers. They can't possibly give you the attention you deserve. We can. When you call us, you talk to people who know your practice and your challenges.

We don't nickel-and-dime you with per-user fees, implementation charges, or hidden costs. We don't lock you into long-term contracts. We earn your business every single month by being the best solution for your practice.

Easy Transition, Real Support

We know switching systems feels daunting. That's why we handle the heavy lifting. Our team migrates your data, trains your staff, and stays by your side through go-live and beyond. Most practices are fully operational within 2-4 weeks.

And our support doesn't end after implementation. Unlimited phone, email, and chat support is included — not as an upsell, but as part of who we are. Real humans, fast responses, no ticket queues.
