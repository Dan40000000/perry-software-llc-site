Our Story

Nuvora Health was born from frustration. We spent months talking to dermatologists across the country — and the frustration was universal. Overpriced systems, terrible support, migrations that dragged on for months, and software that felt like it was designed to bill consulting hours rather than help patients. Every single practice we spoke with had the same story: they were locked into a system they hated, paying too much, and felt like they had nowhere to turn.

Those conversations became the blueprint for Nuvora. Everything we built — from the pricing model to the onboarding process to the support structure — came directly from what those dermatologists told us they wished existed. We set out to solve their problems, and we haven't stopped since.

Based in Lehi, Utah, we're a small team with a simple mission: create practice management software that medical professionals actually enjoy using, at a price that makes sense for independent practices.

Why We're Different

Big EHR companies have thousands of customers. They can't possibly give you the attention you deserve. We can. When you call us, you talk to people who know your practice and your challenges.

We don't nickel-and-dime you with per-user fees or hidden costs. We handle your entire migration for a one-time setup fee — and most practices make that back within just a few months through lower monthly costs alone. After that, you keep the savings every single month.

Easy Transition, Real Support

We know switching systems feels daunting. That's why we handle the heavy lifting. Our team migrates your data, trains your staff, and stays by your side through go-live and beyond. Most practices are fully operational within 2-4 weeks.

And our support doesn't end after implementation. Unlimited phone, email, and chat support is included — not as an upsell, but as part of who we are. Real humans, fast responses, no ticket queues.
