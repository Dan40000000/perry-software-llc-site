SMS Privacy Policy

Effective date: May 4, 2026

This SMS Privacy Policy explains how Perry Software LLC handles information collected in connection with operational text messaging.

What we collect

  • Your mobile phone number
  • Your opt-in and opt-out status
  • Basic message history and delivery status
  • Any information you voluntarily include in an SMS reply or consent request

How we use SMS information

We use SMS-related information to:

  • Send operational text messages you requested or authorized
  • Manage delivery, support, and opt-out requests
  • Maintain compliance records for messaging consent
  • Improve messaging reliability and support workflows

Sharing

We do not sell your SMS consent or phone number. We only share SMS-related information with service providers needed to send and support operational text messages, such as messaging and hosting vendors.

Retention

We retain SMS consent and messaging records for operational, support, compliance, and audit purposes for as long as reasonably necessary.

Your choices

You can opt out of text messages at any time by replying STOP. You may request help by replying HELP, emailing dan@perrysoftwarellc.com, or calling 541-231-8693.

Changes

We may update this policy from time to time. Updated versions will be posted on this page with a revised effective date.
