SMS Opt-In Evidence

This public page documents the operational SMS call to action used by Nuvora Health, operated by Perry Software LLC, so messaging reviewers can verify the consent flow without logging into the patient portal.

Where SMS consent is captured

1. Public SMS preference form

Patients can submit an optional SMS preference at https://perry-software-site.vercel.app/sms-consent.html. The SMS checkbox is optional, unchecked by default, and not required to submit the form.

2. Patient intake, check-in, and portal registration

During intake, check-in, and portal registration, patients provide or verify their mobile number and see the same SMS disclosure before choosing whether to receive operational text messages.

3. Staff-assisted registration

Staff may record the patient preference only after the patient provides a mobile number and confirms the SMS disclosure. Staff can also record that the patient declined SMS.

Exact disclosure shown at opt-in

Optional SMS opt-in: I agree to receive operational SMS messages from Nuvora Health, operated by Perry Software LLC, and its healthcare practice demo environment at the mobile number provided above. I understand that message frequency varies, message and data rates may apply, I can reply HELP for help, and I can reply STOP to opt out at any time. I understand that checking this box is not required for treatment, payment, registration, appointments, or any other service.

Program details

  • Business/operator: Perry Software LLC.
  • Product name shown to patients: Nuvora Health.
  • Use case: operational patient notifications for dermatology care and account workflows.
  • Message types: appointment reminders, scheduling updates, billing notices, prescription coordination, and care follow-up messages.
  • Message frequency varies. Message and data rates may apply.
  • Help: reply HELP, email dan@perrysoftwarellc.com, or call 541-231-8693.
  • Opt-out: reply STOP at any time.
  • SMS consent is optional and is not required for treatment, purchase, payment, appointment scheduling, registration, or any other office service.
